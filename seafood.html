From the Gulf of Kutch
to Reefer Ships Worldwide

Gujarat's 1,600 km coastline — the longest of any Indian state — and the nutrient-rich waters of the Gulf of Kutch make Veraval and Porbandar among India's most productive seafood hubs. Zaffra Agro operates a dedicated MPEDA-approved processing facility in Veraval, sourcing from certified trawlers and registered aquaculture farms across Saurashtra.

Our seafood program handles both wild-caught species — Pomfret, Seer, and squid landed daily at Veraval port — and farmed Vannamei shrimp from certified ponds in Bhavnagar and Jamnagar districts. Every batch is IQF or block-frozen within hours of processing, HACCP-documented, and stored at -18°C through to container loading.

Whether you require retail-ready IQF shrimp for a European supermarket chain, bulk block-frozen fish for Gulf food-service distributors, or private-label packing for an Asian importer, our export desk manages MPEDA documentation, health certification, and cold-chain logistics end to end.

Our Species

Six Export Species,
Processed to HACCP Standard

Each species is processed to strict cold-chain and food-safety protocols, with full traceability from catch or pond to container.

Vannamei Shrimp

India's leading aquaculture export species. Farmed in certified ponds across Bhavnagar and Jamnagar, our Vannamei is harvested, iced, and delivered to the Veraval plant within 4 hours for IQF processing. Available head-on, headless shell-on (HLSO), and peeled & deveined (P&D), graded by count per kilogram.

16/20 – 50/70 Count IQF / Block Frozen Aug – Dec Peak 1 kg / 2 kg Carton

Black Tiger Shrimp

Prized in Japanese and Southeast Asian markets for its firm texture and bold striping, our Black Tiger shrimp is sourced from traditional low-density ponds along the Kutch coast. Slower-grown and larger-bodied than Vannamei, it commands premium positioning in sashimi-grade and tempura applications.

8/12 – 21/25 Count Sashimi Grade Sep – Jan Peak IQF Carton

Pomfret

Wild-caught silver and black Pomfret landed daily at Veraval and Porbandar. Whole-round or dressed, this delicate white-fleshed fish is a staple across Gulf and Southeast Asian food-service menus. Sized and graded by weight, block-frozen in interleaved layers to preserve individual fillet integrity.

200g – 800g Grade Whole / Dressed Oct – May Peak 10 kg Master Carton

Seer Fish (Surmai)

One of India's most sought-after export fin-fish species, prized for its dense, boneless steaks and rich flavour. Caught by registered trawlers operating out of Veraval, Seer fish is processed into whole, gutted, or steak-cut formats to buyer specification and IQF frozen at source.

1 kg – 5 kg Whole Steak Cut Available Nov – Apr Peak 20 kg Master Carton

Squid

Whole-cleaned, tubes, and rings processed from fresh Loligo and Doryteuthis squid landed along the Saurashtra coast. Hand-cleaned to remove skin and cartilage, then IQF frozen to retain the firm, translucent texture demanded by European and East Asian buyers.

Tubes / Rings / Whole Hand Cleaned Year-Round 1 kg / 2 kg Block

Cuttlefish

Whole-cleaned and fillet cuttlefish processed to consistent thickness and colour grade. A strong performer in Mediterranean and East Asian markets, our cuttlefish is block-frozen in glazed layers to protect texture through long-distance sea freight.

Whole / Fillet Glazed Block Frozen Year-Round 10 kg Carton

Our Process

From Trawler & Pond to
Your Cold Store

A rigorous four-stage chain of custody guarantees that the quality we process in Veraval arrives intact at your destination port.

01

Catch & Harvest

Wild species are landed daily by registered trawlers at Veraval and Porbandar; farmed shrimp is harvested from certified ponds and transported live-chilled. All raw material is logged for full batch traceability from source.

02

Grading & Processing

Every batch passes through a HACCP-controlled line: washing, sorting by count/size, deheading or filleting as required, and visual defect inspection. Only export-grade material proceeds to freezing.

03

IQF & Block Freezing

Graded product is individually quick-frozen or plate-frozen to a core temperature of -18°C within four hours of processing, then glazed, weighed, and packed to buyer specification with full lot coding.

04

Cold Chain

Frozen cartons move directly into on-site cold storage and temperature-monitored reefer containers — never broken until the destination cold store. Temperature logs are shared digitally with buyers on arrival.

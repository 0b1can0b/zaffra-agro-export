Our Roots

From the
Fields of
Gujarat.

Founded in 2005, Zaffra Agro Export LLP was built on one conviction: the world deserves to taste India at its best. Gujarat's fertile black-soil plains, its ancient spice trade heritage, and its proximity to the Gulf of Kutch's rich marine ecosystem make it one of the subcontinent's most productive export corridors.

We partner directly with 300+ smallholder farmers across Saurashtra, Mehsana and the Coastal belt — providing them fair trade rates, quality input support, and a guaranteed market, while delivering our buyers the consistency and traceability that global compliance demands.

Certifications & Compliance

Built on
International Standards

APEDA

APEDA Registered

India's Agricultural & Processed Food Products Export Development Authority (APEDA) registered exporter — the standard for Indian agro exports.

ISO

ISO 22000 Certified

ISO 22000:2018 Food Safety Management System certified across all pack-house operations and cold chain handling procedures.

HACCP

HACCP Certified

Hazard Analysis & Critical Control Points certification covering all our seafood and processed product lines to meet EU and USFDA standards.

EU

EU MRL Compliant

All fresh produce consignments are tested against EU Maximum Residue Limits via NABL-accredited laboratories before loading.

FSSAI

FSSAI Licensed

Food Safety and Standards Authority of India licensed operations — ensuring food safety compliance under Indian and international regulations.

MPEDA

MPEDA Approved

Marine Products Export Development Authority (MPEDA) approved seafood processing unit — mandatory for all Indian seafood exporters.

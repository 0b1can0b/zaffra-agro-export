Commodities Born From
India's Richest Soils

Gujarat's agricultural belt — stretching across the Saurashtra plains, the Banas river basin, and the fertile black-soil tracts of Kutch — is one of India's most productive farming regions. The combination of rich alluvial soils, abundant sunshine, and optimal irrigation creates ideal conditions for premium grains, oilseeds, and cash crops that meet the most rigorous international quality benchmarks.

Zaffra Agro Export LLP works directly with a curated network of over 300 farmer-partners across Gujarat, Rajasthan, and Madhya Pradesh, ensuring traceability from field to shipment. Every consignment undergoes multi-stage cleaning, grading, and quality testing before packing — a process that has earned us consistent repeat orders from buyers in the Middle East, Africa, Southeast Asia, and Europe.

We supply rice, wheat, sesame seeds, peanuts, and edible oils in bulk and consumer-pack formats, customised to destination-market regulations and buyer specifications.

Our Process

From Field to Freight —
The Zaffra Method

A rigorous four-stage chain of custody that maintains commodity integrity from farm procurement to container loading at JNPT, Mundra, or Kandla.

01

Procurement

Direct sourcing from 300+ verified farmer-partners and APMC mandis across Gujarat, Rajasthan, and Madhya Pradesh. Farm-level quality audits and pre-purchase sampling ensure only conforming lots enter our supply chain.

02

Cleaning & Grading

Multi-stage mechanical cleaning via rotary sieves, destoners, and aspirators removes foreign matter and damaged grain. Sortex colour-sorting machines reject discoloured kernels to achieve high visual purity standards.

03

Quality Testing

Every lot is tested in our ISO-calibrated in-house laboratory for moisture, purity, oil content, protein, aflatoxin, pesticide residues, and heavy metals. Third-party verification by SGS or Bureau Veritas available on request.

04

Export Packing

Packing in buyer-specified PP woven, HDPE, jute, or retail bags at our FSSAI-licensed facility. Full phytosanitary certification, certificate of origin, and APEDA quality certification provided with every consignment.

Packaging & Logistics

Ready for Global Shipping

We manage all logistics from our Gujarat warehouses to your destination port — bulk, break-bulk, and containerised shipments in FCL and LCL formats.

PP Woven & HDPE Bags

Standard 25 kg and 50 kg PP woven or HDPE bags with UV-treated outer layer for transit durability. Custom printing with buyer brand, batch number, country of origin, and nutritional data available on all commodities.

Container & Flexi-Tank

20' and 40' FCL containers for grains and oilseeds. Flexi-tanks (24,000 litres) for edible oil shipments. Containers are fumigated, moisture-protected, and sealed per destination phytosanitary requirements before stuffing.

Documentation & Compliance

Complete export documentation: Commercial Invoice, Packing List, Bill of Lading, Certificate of Origin, Phytosanitary Certificate, APEDA Certification, and all destination-country compliance documents per shipment.

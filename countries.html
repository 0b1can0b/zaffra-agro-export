Market Detail

Where We Export

Each market has unique preferences, compliance requirements and import cycles. We are well-versed in all of them.

🇦🇪 Gulf & Middle East — Primary Market

Gulf Cooperation Council

UAE Saudi Arabia Qatar Kuwait Oman Bahrain
Top exports: Alphonso & Kesar mangoes, Red onions, Vannamei shrimp, Cumin, Coriander, Basmati rice, Pomegranates, Bananas.

The Gulf accounts for over 60% of our fresh fruit and vegetable tonnage. With Indian expat populations exceeding 3.5 million in the UAE alone, demand for authentic fresh produce is consistent year-round. We maintain regular reefer container schedules to all major Gulf ports: Jebel Ali, Dammam, Hamad, Shuwaikh and Sohar.
🇬🇧 Europe — Premium Market

United Kingdom & Europe

United Kingdom Netherlands Germany Belgium
Top exports: Fresh mangoes (UKCA/EU phytosanitary compliant), Frozen seafood (HACCP), Spices (EU MRL compliant), Sesame seeds, Fresh vegetables.

European buyers demand full traceability, GlobalG.A.P. or equivalent, and strict MRL testing. All our European shipments are backed by NABL-accredited lab reports, phytosanitary certificates and EU-format health certificates. Arrival ports: Felixstowe, Rotterdam (distribution hub), Hamburg.
🇸🇬 South & South-East Asia — Growing Market

South & South-East Asia

Singapore Malaysia Sri Lanka Bangladesh
Top exports: Basmati & non-basmati rice, Spices (cumin, coriander), Seafood (frozen IQF shrimp), Fresh vegetables, Peanuts, Sesame seeds.

South-East Asia's growing Indian diaspora and thriving hospitality sector creates steady demand for authentic Indian agro products. Singapore and Malaysia function as regional distribution hubs from which goods reach Indonesia, Thailand and Vietnam. We offer FCL and LCL shipments via Chennai and Mundra ports.

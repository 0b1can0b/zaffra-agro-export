Accreditations

Our Certifications

APEDA

APEDA Registered Exporter

Registered with India's Agricultural & Processed Food Products Export Development Authority under the Agricultural Products (Grading & Marketing) Act. All fresh produce exports are APEDA certified.

ISO

ISO 22000:2018 — Food Safety

International Food Safety Management System certification covering all our pack-house operations, cold chain procedures, and product handling — audited annually by a third-party certifying body.

HACCP

HACCP Certified

Hazard Analysis & Critical Control Points system implemented across all seafood processing, fresh produce packing and dry goods handling. Required for EU and US market access.

FSSAI

FSSAI License

Food Safety and Standards Authority of India central license covering manufacturing, processing, storage and distribution of food products for export. License No: 10018012000000.

MPEDA

MPEDA Approved Processing Unit

Marine Products Export Development Authority approved seafood processing facility — mandatory for all Indian seafood exporters. Approval covers shrimp, fish, squid and cephalopods.

EU

EU MRL & GlobalG.A.P.

All fresh produce for European markets is tested against EU Maximum Residue Limits at NABL-accredited laboratories. Selected fruit lines carry GlobalG.A.P. certification covering farm-level Good Agricultural Practices.

How We Ensure Quality

The Zaffra
Quality Chain

01

Farm Audit & Approval

Each supplying farm is audited annually for pesticide usage, water quality, soil health and worker practices. Only approved farms enter our supply chain. Non-compliant farms are immediately suspended.

02

Field & Pack-House Grading

Produce is graded for size, weight, brix (sugar content), colour and physical defects using calibrated mechanical graders. Rejection rates of 15–25% at this stage ensure only premium product ships.

03

Pre-Shipment Lab Testing

Random lot samples are sent to NABL-accredited third-party laboratories for pesticide residue (500+ compounds), microbiological testing, heavy metals and market-specific MRL compliance. Reports issued within 48 hours.

04

Cold Chain & Loading

Pre-cooled produce is loaded into reefer containers with calibrated temperature loggers. Container temperatures are monitored remotely throughout transit. Buyers receive the temperature log on arrival.

Third-Party
Lab Verification

We do not operate our own laboratory — deliberately. By partnering with NABL-accredited third-party labs, we ensure complete independence between the testing authority and the exporter. Our buyers receive impartial, legally recognised lab reports with every shipment.

Our laboratory partners include SGS India, Bureau Veritas, and Intertek — globally recognised inspection and certification organisations.

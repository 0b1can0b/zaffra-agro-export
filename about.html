Our Story

Rooted in
Gujarat.
Reaching the World.

Two decades of earning trust, one consignment at a time.

Est. 2005

Built on a Simple
Conviction

Zaffra Agro Export LLP was founded by Dhruv Patel in 2005 with a single container of Kesar mangoes shipped to Dubai. That first shipment — and the relationship it forged with a Deira wholesale importer — became the template for everything that followed: quality that speaks, relationships that endure, and service that never wavers.

Twenty years on, Zaffra exports across 5 product categories to 15+ countries. We have grown from a three-person operation in Maninagar to a 45-member company with dedicated pack-houses in Anand and Surat, a seafood processing unit near Veraval, and a spice cleaning and grading facility in Unjha.

"Zaffra" — the Gujarati word for saffron — was chosen deliberately. Saffron is precious, cultivated with enormous care, and celebrated the world over. That is the standard we hold ourselves to: not the cheapest, but the most trusted.

What Drives Us

Three Principles.
Everything Else Follows.

01

Farm-forward Sourcing

We work directly with 300+ growers across Saurashtra, the Coastal Belt, and north Gujarat — paying farmgate rates 15–20% above APMC average, providing quality inputs, and guaranteeing off-take. Better farmer economics mean better quality in the field.

02

Uncompromised Quality

Every export lot is tested at NABL-accredited laboratories before loading. We refuse to ship anything that wouldn't pass on arrival — our rejection rate at destination is under 0.3%, one of the lowest in the Indian agro-export industry.

03

Long-term Partnership

Over 70% of our buyer accounts have been active for more than 5 years. We grow with our buyers — offering forward price agreements during peak seasons, priority booking on reefer containers, and dedicated relationship managers for every account.

Our Journey

Two Decades
of Growth

2005

The First Container

Dhruv Patel ships the first container — Kesar mangoes to Dubai's Deira wholesale market. The relationship establishes Zaffra's reputation for consistency and reliability in the Gulf.

2009

APEDA Registration & Spice Division

Formal APEDA registration obtained. Spice export division launched from Unjha — India's largest cumin and fennel trading hub. First European buyer (Netherlands) onboarded.

2013

Seafood Entry & MPEDA Approval

Seafood processing unit established near Veraval port — the heart of Gujarat's fishing industry. MPEDA approval obtained. First IQF shrimp export to Singapore.

2018

ISO 22000 & Pack-House Expansion

ISO 22000:2018 certification achieved across all facilities. New 12,000 sq.ft pack-house in Anand commissioned with pre-cooling chambers and electronic sorting lines. EU market share doubles.

2026

15+ Countries, 200+ SKUs

Today, Zaffra serves buyers in 15+ countries across the Gulf, Europe and South-East Asia with a product portfolio of 200+ SKUs. Ongoing expansion into UK market post-UKCA alignment and new agro-commodity lines in sesame and peanuts.

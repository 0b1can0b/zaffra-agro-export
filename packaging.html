Cold Chain Integrity

Temperature-
Controlled from
Farm to Port

01

Field Pre-Cooling

Within 2–4 hours of harvest, produce enters our forced-air pre-cooling tunnels — reducing pulp temperature to the target range of 8–12°C (fruits) or 4–6°C (leafy vegetables) before packing begins.

02

Pack-House Environment

All packing operations take place in climate-controlled environments at 12–15°C. Workers handle produce with nitrile gloves. Packing lines are HACCP-compliant with continuous temperature monitoring.

03

Reefer Container Loading

Pre-cooled containers (2°C to 14°C depending on product) are loaded using refrigerated dock bays. Each container is fitted with a temperature data logger recording at 15-minute intervals throughout the voyage.

04

Transit Monitoring

Remote reefer tracking is active from port of origin to destination. Buyers receive real-time vessel ETAs and temperature log summary reports on request. Any deviation triggers immediate carrier notification.

Freight Management

We Handle
the Complexity

Our in-house logistics team manages all aspects of freight coordination — container booking, phytosanitary clearance, customs documentation, and freight forwarding — so you don't have to.

We maintain forward container space agreements with major reefer carriers including Maersk, MSC, CMA CGM and Hapag-Lloyd, giving us priority access during high-season demand peaks (mango season: March–June).

Full Documentation Suite

Commercial Invoice, Packing List, Bill of Lading, Phytosanitary Certificate, Certificate of Origin, Health Certificate — all prepared in-house and dispatched digitally with every shipment.

24-Hour Enquiry Response

All new enquiries receive a formal response with availability, grade specifications and indicative pricing within one business day. Pre-shipment samples dispatched within 3 working days of request.

Live Shipment Tracking

Buyers receive vessel tracking links on bill of lading issuance. ETA updates are communicated proactively. Arrival notifications and temperature log delivery are standard for all reefer shipments.

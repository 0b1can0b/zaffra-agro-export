Common Questions

What Buyers Often Ask

What is the minimum order quantity?

For fresh fruits and vegetables, our standard MOQ is one full reefer container (typically 14–20 MT depending on the product). For spices and dry commodities, one FCL (20-foot container, approx. 18–22 MT). Sample shipments of 50–200 kg can be arranged via air courier for new buyers.

How long does shipment take?

Transit time depends on the destination. Gulf ports (Jebel Ali, Dammam): 7–12 days. European ports (Rotterdam, Felixstowe): 18–24 days. Singapore / Malaysia: 10–15 days. We maintain forward booking with major reefer carriers to minimise vessel delays.

What documentation do you provide?

Complete export documentation including: Commercial Invoice, Packing List, Bill of Lading, Phytosanitary Certificate, Certificate of Origin (APEDA / Chamber of Commerce), NABL-accredited lab test reports, and market-specific health certificates (EU, USFDA, etc.).

Can we get pre-shipment samples?

Yes — for new buyers placing orders above 5 MT, we offer pre-shipment samples of approximately 2–5 kg (fresh produce) or 500g–1 kg (spices / dry goods) at no charge, with the buyer covering courier costs. Samples match the grade and variety of the final consignment.

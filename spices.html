Gujarat is home to Unjha — Asia's largest spice trading and processing hub, and the epicentre of India's cumin and fennel trade. Zaffra Agro procures directly from Unjha's wholesale market and surrounding growing belts of Banaskantha and Mehsana, working with registered commission agents and farmer-producer organisations we have partnered with for over a decade.

Every lot is cleaned through multi-stage sortex colour-sorting, sieved to size specification, and tested for pesticide residue, aflatoxin, and microbial load at NABL-accredited laboratories before packing. Our facility handles whole seed spices, ground powders, and steam-sterilised formats for buyers requiring reduced microbial counts.

Whether you need container-load whole cumin for a Middle Eastern distributor, ground turmeric for a European spice blender, or steam-sterilised chilli for a North American retailer, our export desk manages Spice Board documentation, lab certification, and shipping end to end.

Cumin Seeds

Gujarat produces over 70% of India's cumin, and Unjha is its trading capital. Our Europe and Singapore-quality cumin is machine-cleaned to 99.5% purity, sortex colour-sorted, and available in whole seed or ground format. Distinctive high oil content delivers the aromatic intensity global spice blenders demand.

99.5% Purity Europe / Singapore Grade Feb – Apr Peak 25 kg PP Bag

Coriander Seeds

Sourced from the black-cotton-soil belts of Gujarat and Rajasthan, our coriander is offered in Eagle and Scooter trade grades, split or whole. Naturally high in volatile oil, it is sortex-cleaned and screened to uniform size for both whole-spice and oleoresin-extraction buyers.

Eagle / Scooter Grade Whole / Split Feb – Mar Peak 25 kg PP Bag

Turmeric

Finger and powder turmeric with curcumin content verified by lab assay, sourced from Gujarat and neighbouring Maharashtra growing belts. Polished finger turmeric and steam-sterilised powder are both available, with curcumin levels declared per lot for buyers with specification requirements.

Curcumin 2–5% Finger / Powder Jan – Mar Peak 25 kg Bag

Red Chilli

Whole and powdered red chilli sourced from Gujarat's Saurashtra belt and neighbouring Andhra Pradesh growing regions, offered across a range of Scoville heat grades and colour values (ASTA). Steam sterilisation available on request to reduce microbial load for retail-ready packing.

ASTA 90–120 Whole / Powder Dec – Feb Peak 25 kg Bag

Green Cardamom

Bold green cardamom sourced from the Idukki and Kodagu hill estates via our trading partnerships, hand-graded for pod size and colour retention. A premium line for Gulf and export markets where large, deep-green pods command a significant price premium.

Bold Green Grade Hand Graded Oct – Feb Peak 5 kg / 10 kg Box

Fenugreek Seeds

Bitter-aromatic fenugreek seed, a Gujarat staple crop grown alongside cumin in the same Banaskantha belt, is machine-cleaned and screened for uniform amber colour. Popular in spice blends, seasonings, and the growing functional-food and nutraceutical segment.

Amber Grade Machine Cleaned Feb – Mar Peak 25 kg / 50 kg Bag

Our Process

From Mandi Floor to
Sealed Container

A rigorous four-stage chain of custody guarantees that the purity we procure at Unjha arrives intact at your destination port.

01

Procurement

Spices are procured lot-by-lot from Unjha's wholesale market and registered farmer-producer organisations, with each lot assessed for moisture, foreign matter, and visual quality before purchase.

02

Sortex & Sieving

Raw material passes through multi-pass sortex colour-sorting, sieving, and destoning lines to remove foreign matter and off-colour material, lifting purity to export-grade specification.

03

Testing & Grading

Every processed lot is sampled and lab-tested for pesticide residue, aflatoxin, and microbial load, then graded to buyer specification — whole, split, or ground — with a Certificate of Analysis issued per batch.

04

Pack & Ship

Graded spice is packed into PP bags, jute bags, or laminated pouches per buyer specification, sealed, and containerised with full Spice Board and phytosanitary documentation for destination clearance.
